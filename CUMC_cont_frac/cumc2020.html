Continued Fractions: How to Understand Self-Similar and Infinite Processes (CUMC 2020)

During the summer of 2020, I attended my first ever Math Conference. The Canadian Undergraduate Mathematics Conference is an annual event held by undergrads with an interest in mathematics. Everything was virtual but the experience was still memorable. I met other undergrads and connected with people that shared a common interest. I chose to present a talk for the 4 day conference and now, after a year, I've decided to make a revision and put it on the Internet. The purpose of the talk is to use Continued Fractions as an analogy to understand infinite processes, which can be surprisingly non-intuitive. Included is a video presentation followed by written material.
